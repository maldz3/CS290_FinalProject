About Me

My interest in cooking developed organically from watching and tasting the delicious and surprisingly healthy food that my family would cook. After getting married and moving to the US, I discovered a whole new world of international cuisine. I'd like to share some of the recipes that were passed down to me from my grandmothers and from my husband's family. Some of these I have modified and experimented with for taste and ease of making. Come and make delicious South Asian cuisine with my easy to follow recipes. Oh and Middle-Earth is a place I would live if I could so you may see some random LOTR fan art :)
